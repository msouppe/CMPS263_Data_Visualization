GIRLS WHO CODE

Technology jobs are among the fastest growing in the country, yet girls are being left behind. While interest in computer science declines over time, the biggest drop off happens between the ages of 13-17. More and more schools are introducing Computer Science curriculum at an ealier age which trends of young females participating in Computer Science courses and programs at different age levels and locations between the K-12 grades have been steadily increasing.
